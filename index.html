Renan Guarese

HCI researcher and PhD candidate in Computer Science at RMIT - Australia. Obtained his MSc. degree in Computer Science at UFRGS, studying Human-Computer interaction. Tutored different courses in HCI, game development and Mixed Reality. Worked in several HCI-related projects in Augmented and Virtual Reality, including a Situated Visualization of Electromagnetic Compatibility project at Halmstad University, in Sweden, and an AR-based platform for the teaching of physics, at UFRGS. Mainly uses Unity, Android, and AR/VR devices for his applications. Also holds a BSc. in Computer Science from UFRGS, having spent one year abroad via the Science without Borders program, at Radford University, in the USA, and a technician degree in IT, from IFRS.
