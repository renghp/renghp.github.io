Renan Guarese

Postdoctoral Researcher in Human-Computer Interaction at KTH - Sweden, specifically working with data visualization in AR/VR for a pharmaceutical manufacturing scenario, partnered with AstraZeneca. PhD candidate in Computer Science at RMIT - Australia. Obtained his MSc. degree in Computer Science at UFRGS - Brazil, studying Human-Computer interaction. Taught courses in HCI, AI, Mixed Reality, and game and web development. Worked in several HCI-related projects in AR/VR, including audio interfaces for visually impaired people, at RMIT, situated visualization of electromagnetic fields at Halmstad University - Sweden, and an AR-based platform for the teaching of physics, at UFRGS. Mainly uses Unity, Android, and AR/VR devices for his applications. Also holds an IT degree from IFRS - Brazil, and a BSc. in Computer Science from UFRGS, with a sandwich period at Radford University and the Illinois Institute of Technology - USA.
